Author, Writer, Media Personality and the UK’s Youngest Teacher

Edvin Zuckovs is a Young Author, Writer, Media Personality and The UK’s Youngest Teacher who is known for his first book, ‘Young Life’ which was published when he was 13 years old, making him one go the UK’s youngest authors. (Google Verification)

Edvin was named the UK’s Youngest Teacher by the British Press, The Daily Mirror, The Sun, The Lincs Online and more, in 2024.

On the 3rd of June 2021, ‘Young Life’ by Edvin Zuckovs (aged 13) was published in the United States of America and is now sold globally on Amazon, Waterstones, Walmart, Harvard Book Store and more.

In 2021, Edvin received his official Google Verification badge, after his first book winning global recognition of national book stores around the world. His works with celebrities, editors and companies appearing on radio breakfast shows and taking part in events was one of his main aspects to the start to his public career.

In an interview Edvin said:

“One of my quotes I used In my first book was ‘The things you do now will always matter in the future, opening more doors with adventures’ and is something I have always stuck with.”
